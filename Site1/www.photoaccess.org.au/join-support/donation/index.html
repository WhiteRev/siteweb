Support us

PhotoAccess is a non-profit organisation and a registered charity, and with your support we can continue our work. For over thirty years PhotoAccess has provided a place for creation conversation, educations and exhibitions accessible to all within our community. Donations of $2 or more are tax deductible, and support exhibitions, artist residencies, community projects and public access programs.

No amount is too small and all funds raised help us to ensure photo-based art has a place to call home.

Equipment & book donations 

We gladly receive donations of photographic equipment for use in our workshops and projects.
Film cameras, in particular medium format cameras, are welcomed.
Photography and art books for our library are also welcome. If you would like to donate equipment or books please email or call us.
