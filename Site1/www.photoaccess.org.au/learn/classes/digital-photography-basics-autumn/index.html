Digital Photography Basics: Autumn

About this workshop 

Learn everything you need to know about getting your digital camera off automatic mode and take your photography to the next level!

In this hands-on practical workshop, you will gain a practical understanding of your camera’s functions – aperture, shutter speed and ISO – together with manual focus techniques and the basics of creative composition. You’ll apply these practical techniques and newly established skills over two three hour sessions and have the opportunity to ask questions and gain feedback, all under the guidance of experienced photographer and tutor, Jane Duong.

This workshop is for absolute beginners with a digital single-lens reflex (DSLR) cameras or mirror-less cameras (with interchangeable lens) wishing to gain an understanding of the basic principles of digital photography.

Date, time & location 

Digital Photography Basics: Autumn, will take place at PhotoAccess on Saturday 18 May, 10am – 4pm.
PhotoAccess is located within the Manuka Arts Centre,
Corner Manuka Circle & New South Wales Cres,
Griffith ACT (next door to the Manuka Pool)

What will be provided? 

Notebook, pencil & workshop notes

What to bring? 

  • Your DSLR or Compact camera
  • Charged batteries
  • Memory card
  • Camera Manual (if you have it)
