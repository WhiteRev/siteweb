Bakelite: Shined-up and looking right

Bakelite: Shined-up and looking right 

In partnership with Canberra Museum and Gallery (CMAG), your kids can spend two days in the 1960s and discover why grandma is always going on about her old camera!

On this fun weekend course exploring the mid-twentieth century history of photography, you will learn about vintage cameras, try shooting with antiquated film formats and hand process your own prints using period specific darkroom papers.

This workshop is for ages 13-17 year olds, no photography experience is necessary. Enrol now!

Time & Date  

Bakelite will run over two consecutive day, Saturday 15 June – 16 June, 10:00am – 4:00 pm each day.
