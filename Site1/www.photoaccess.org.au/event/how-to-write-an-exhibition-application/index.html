How to write an exhibition application

Are you working on a proposal for an exhibition? Need some expert advise?  Then come along to our FREE How to write an exhibition proposal session with Kate Murphy (AKA Ellis Hutch), artist, lecture at the ANU SOA&D and experienced gallery professional.  The session will include tips on how to write and structure a competitive exhibition proposal, and include a discussion of successful applications.

Running Thursday 2 May 2019 from 6:00 – 7:00pm at PhotoAccess. Free event, bookings essential as places are limited.
