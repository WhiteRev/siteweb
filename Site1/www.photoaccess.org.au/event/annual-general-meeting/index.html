Annual General Meeting

PhotoAccess Incorporated
6pm Wednesday 8 May 2019
PhotoAccess Teaching Room, Manuka Arts Centre
Cnr Manuka Circle & New South Crescent, Griffith ACT 2603

PhotoAccess will be holding it’s Annual General Meeting (AGM) at 6pm on Thursday 8 May 2019 and invites all Members, new and returning, to come along and engage with the Board and Director, Kirsten Wehner, to discuss the direction of PhotoAccess.

AGENDA

  1. Receive apologies
  2. Confirm the minutes of the 2018 Annual General Meeting
  3. Receive and adopt the Financial Statements for the year ended 31 December 2018, including the balance sheet and net profit or loss for the financial year and the auditor’s report
  4. Receive the Annual Report 2018 and note principal activities during the year
  5. Conduct an election for a new board of between seven and 10 members, including Chair, Deputy Chair, Treasurer, Secretary and three to six ordinary members
  6. Appoint the auditor for 2019
  7. Other business

If you would like to nominate yourself or another member for the Board, please find the Board Nomination Form below.

If you are unable to make the AGM but would like to have a proxy vote, please find the Proxy Vote Form below.
